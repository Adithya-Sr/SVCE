BRUSH FOOTED BUTTERFLIES-NYMPHALIDAE

ANGLED CASTOR

Ariadne ariadne

Habits

Weak fliers; fly close to the ground, often sailing and halting for rest; found in open areas

Description

Upperside is ochraceous rufous with black wavy lines. Fore wing is truncated at the apex with a conspicuous white spot. Angulated tornus. Underside tinted with brown having chestnut-brown markings

BLUE PANSY

Junonia orithya

Habits

Swift fliers, active during hottest part of the day; fly close to the ground. Rest on the ground or foliage with wings spread wide.

Description

Brilliant blue coloured upperside with two eye spots on hind wings. Brown and black fore wings with two white bands and two eye spots. Underside is greyish brown with wavy lines

BLUE TIGER

Tirumala limniace

Habits

Slow undulating flight; flutters and sails periodically. Frequently settlings on flowers

Description

Large wings with a ground colour of black or dark brown marked with blue streaks. Underside is paler but pattern is the same on both sides

BUSHBROWN SPECIES

Mycalesis

Habits

Unstable flight and flies close to the ground. Difficult to follow

Description

Upperside is brown. Underside has a white line that cuts across both wings enclosing all eye spots of varying sizes

CHOCOLATE PANSY

Junonia iphita

Habits

Active and territorial. Found in wooded areas and forests. Fly low and spend long periods basking on foliage or ground

Description

Chocolate brown coloured with darker markings on the fore wings and eye spots on the hind wings. During dry seasons underside looks like dry leaf forming an excellent mimic

COMMON CROW

Euploea core

Habits

Smooth and effortless sailing flight. Mud-puddles and migrates locally. Gather together in large numbers for communal roosting in semi-shaded areas

Description

Upperside dark brown and underside pale brown. Two series of white spots run along the margin of the wings

COMMON EVENING BROWN

Melanitis leda

Habits

Crepuscular, erratic flight, close to the ground. Hard to spot among dead leaves. Feeds on rotten fruits

Description

Dark brown upperside bearing two black spots inwardly shaded with orange on fore wing apex, each with a white pupil. In the wet season, underside is dark brown marked with series of white pupiled eye spots unlike dry season which has fewer markings.

GREAT EGGFLY

Hypolimnas bolina

Habits

Seen in wet forests and in gardens too; strong on the wings. Migratory. Terretorial males

Description

Jet black male marginated with white dots; upperside has oval white spots on each wing tinged with purple; larger spot on hind wing. Females mimic Common Crow except they are larger in size with notched white spots and bluish markings. Underside is dark brown for both sexes

GREY PANSY

Junonia atlites

Habits

Flight is swift, sailing, jerky and close to the ground. Prefers bright sunlight and to glide over meadows and near water bodies

Description

Upperside is pale greyish buff with dark brown lines; marginated with complete round discal eyespots on both wings. Underside is pale with faint markings

JOKER

Byblia ilithyia

Habits

The flight is weak, fluttering. While resting they open and close their wings. They fly close to the ground and settle on low vegetation, with their wings held open

Description

Male has deep rich orange upperside with three narrow short black bands. Underside is pale orange. Female is similar, except a paler upperside and dark brown markings

LEMON PANSY

Junonia lemonias

Habits

Active butterfly seen basking on dried leaves and twigs; flies quickly close to the ground and rests with its wings open

Description

Upperside is brown coloured with eye spots as well as black and lemon yellow spots. Underside is yellowish brown with wavy lines and dots. In the dry season markings are dull and in the wet season they are conspicuous

PEACOCK PANSY

Junonia almana

Habits

Swift flight and flies close to the ground; territorial and found in sunlit grassy patches near forest edges and water bodies

Description

Upperside is tawny orange with noticeable eyespots on each wing; a large eyespot on the hind wing and two smaller spots on the fore wing. Underside is pale orange with wavy patterns

PLAIN TIGER

Danaus chrysippus

Habits

Slow, straight and laborious flight close to the ground with occasional deviations

Description

Tawny, medium-sized butterfly. Fore wing has black border with white bands near the tip. Hind wing has thin black border with white spots and three small spots near centre. Upperside is brighter than the reverse. Similar to Striped Tiger but lacks the rich colouration and broad black stripes on the wings

STRIPED TIGER

Danaus genutia

Habits

Strong fliers and mostly remain close to the ground. Stronger and faster wing beats than Plain Tiger

Description

Wings are tawny with black and broad veins. Wing margins are black with two series of white spots. It has white markings towards the tip. Underside is similar but paler

TAWNY COSTER

Acraea violae

Habits

Sluggish, long-sustained flight close to the ground. Prefers open areas and frequently visits flowers

Description

Upperside is brick red and has spotted wings with a black border. Hind wing has a thick black border with obscure white spots. Underside is paler with hind wing having larger, clearly visible white spots. It plays dead when attacked
