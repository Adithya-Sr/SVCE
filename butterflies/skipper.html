Skippers-HESPERIIDAE

COMMON BANDED AWL

Hasora chromus

Habits

Flight is swift, strong, skipping and fluttering. Remains close to the ground

Description

Upperside is velvety brown with white spots. Underside has purple tinge with white band on hind wing

ORIENTAL GRASS DART

Taractrocera maevius

Habits

Flight is weak and low

Description

Small skipper with white semi-transparent spots and a white streak on the upperside of the fore wing. Underside of the hind wing is ochreous brown having pale brown veins with a white outline. Both the sides of the hind wing have a discal series of white spots

SWIFT SPECIES

Habits

Flight is swift, smooth and rapid, settles frequently and prefers open sunny places; flies close to the ground

Description

A small brown butterfly with semi-transparent spots present on the fore wings and hind wings
